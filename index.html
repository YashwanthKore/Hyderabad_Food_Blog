Food items to taste in Hyderabad

Hyderbad is very popular for different kinds of dishes, Hyderabadi Biryani is one of the most popular dishes of the city and it is originated from originating from the kitchens of the Nizams of Hyderabad. There are certain food items which are so special that it is absolutely essential for you to not miss them, especially when you happen visit Hyderabad. The cuisine of Hyderabad is so special and rarely fail to amaze anyone. Don't forget to taste these foods when you visit hyderabad:

1. Biryani

About:

The word 'biryani' is derived from a Persian word, birian, which means fried before cooking. It is made with basmati rice and goat meat or chicken and cooked with the dum pukht method. Originating in the kitchens of the Nizam of Hyderabad.

2. Mirchi Ka Salan

About:

Mirchi ka salan is a traditional Hyderabadi dish prepared on special occasions. The mirchi (chilli peppers) are cooked in spices and mixed with a ground peanut paste which gives the dish a grainy texture.

3. Haleem

About:

Hyderabadi haleem first made its appearance in the royal kitchen of the Nizam. It is seasonal delicacy and mostly found during the Ramzon season. This dish is slow cooked for seven to eight hours and then vigorously stirred or beaten with a pestle-like stirring stick.

4. Irani chai & Osmania Biscuits

About:

Irani chai is very tastefully made in near ancient big vessels goes well with almost anything and it is best enjoyed with the special Hyderabadi Osmania biscuits.It is believed to be brought to Hyderabad by Persian immigrants who landed at Mumbai port and then migrated to Hyderabad.

5. Kheema Somasa

About:

Keema means mince of anything. The samosa is claimed to have originated in Central Asia. Kheema Samosa filling can be anything, the most common being potato, and peas filling, or ground meat.
