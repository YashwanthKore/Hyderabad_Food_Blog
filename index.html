Food items to taste in Hyderabad

Hyderbad is very popular for different kinds of dishes, Hyderabadi Biryani is one of the most popular dishes of the city. There are certain food items which are so special that it is essential for you to not miss them, especially when you happen visit Hyderabad don't forget to taste these foods:

1. Biryani

About:

Biryani is the most popular food and people will really go crazy for it in Hyderbad. Biryani is an persian word which means "fried before cooking". Generally it is made with basmati rice and any of these items(chicken, goat meat, fish or egg)

2. Mirchi Ka Salan

About:

Mirchi ka salan is made with Chilli peppers and ground peanut paste. It is usually made when there is an traditional occassions. People will have to taste Mirchi ka salan with Biryani combo alteast once in their life time.

3. Haleem

About:

Haleem is the most delicious dish that is only available in the season of ramzon in Hyderabad. Generally it is made with goat meat or chicken. It takes almost eight hours to cook.

4. Irani chai & Osmania Biscuits

About:

People in Hyderabad will go crazy for for Irani chai and loves to have Osmania biscuits with it.The Irani chai is made so taste so tastefully with the ancient technique.

5. Kheema Somasa

About:

In Hyderabad people are so fond of Kheema Samosa for its taste and the price of it is also very less. The word Kheema means mince of anything, generally in Hyderabad kheema will be of potatos, peas or chicken meat.
